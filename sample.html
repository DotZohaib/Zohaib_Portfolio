Problem: Email link not opening email client

Your mailto link isn't working because of one of these common issues:

  • No default email client configured on your device
  • Browser restrictions or security settings
  • Corporate network policies blocking mailto links
  • Outdated browser or operating system

Manual Setup Instructions

If nothing works, you may need to set up a default email client:

  • Windows: Go to Settings > Apps > Default apps > Email, and choose your preferred email client
  • Mac: Go to Mail > Preferences > General > Default email reader
  • Chrome: Type chrome://settings/handlers in address bar and ensure mailto is allowed
